WELCOME TO
HOLLY ANGELS SCHOOL

A Great Place For Education

Holy Angels School was started at Rajpura, district Patiala, in the state of Punjab, in July 1975. It is managed by Holy Angels (a registered Catholic Lay organization). At present the school is up to standard X and is affiliated with council for the Indian School certificate Examination, New Delhi, sin 1984.

Good education provides the greatest opportunity for life. Education has often remained a personal affair, a private investment for one’s own purposes. On the other hand, the Kothari Commission laid greed stress on the necessary dimensions of education-one aimed at personal development and the other at social commitment. This openness, awareness and response to the needs and concerns of others, in the neighborhood, in the town and in the country, will be a feature that this school will underscore.
